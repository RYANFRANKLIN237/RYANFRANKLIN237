## 🎓 InstaKnow — My Flagship Product

**InstaKnow** is a bilingual (French & English) AI-powered study guide app built specifically for university students in **Cameroon and West/Central Africa**. It is live on the Google Play Store.

> *The best AI study companion built for African university students.*

### What InstaKnow offers:
- 🤖 **AI Tutors per subject** — personalized guided learning powered by artificial intelligence
- 📝 **Practice Quizzes** — exam-focused knowledge testing
- 🎯 **Focus Mode** — blocks distracting notifications so students can study without interruption
- ⏰ **Daily Reminders** — builds consistent study habits through smart scheduling
- 📊 **Progress Tracking** — visual dashboards showing learning improvement over time
- 🇫🇷🇬🇧 **Fully Bilingual** — French and English, serving both Francophone and Anglophone African students

**[⬇️ Download InstaKnow on Google Play](https://tinyurl.com/InstaKnow)**
